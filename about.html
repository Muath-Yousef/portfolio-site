About

Cybersecurity Engineer — security automation, workflows, and practical tooling.

Background

I am a Cybersecurity Engineer with a practical focus: building systems that detect threats, automate responses, and reduce the manual overhead that slows security teams down. My work lives at the intersection of engineering and operations.

Over the years I have developed tooling, designed pipelines, and built workflows that solve concrete problems — not exercises in compliance theatre. I prefer working with clear requirements, measurable outcomes, and code that can be read by the next person on the team.

I am the architect of Project Synapse, a framework for connecting security intelligence and operational workflows into a coherent, maintainable system. Synapse represents the accumulated thinking across many engagements and is still in active development.

I am building toward Kyrie as a future brand direction for independent security engineering and product work. The operating structure is still in development.
