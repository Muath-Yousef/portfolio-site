Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

Do you work with clients outside Jordan?

Yes — I work remotely with clients globally. Most of my engagements are fully remote. If on-site work is required, we can discuss that in scoping.

What types of engagements do you take on?

Security architecture design, SIEM engineering, penetration testing, cloud security hardening, security automation, and awareness training. See the Services page for full detail. I don't take on pure documentation engagements without implementation.

How long does a typical engagement take?

Depends on scope. A focused vulnerability assessment might be 1–2 weeks. A SIEM deployment for an SME is typically 4–8 weeks. Architecture design engagements vary. I'll give you a realistic timeline in scoping — not a padded one.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, standard NDAs are fine. Confidentiality is assumed for all client work — what's listed publicly on this site represents patterns and architecture approaches, never client-specific data or deliverables.

What's the best way to start?

LinkedIn or email with a brief description of your problem. I'll respond with a direct fit/no-fit assessment and, if it's a good fit, a proposed next step. No sales calls, no discovery decks — just a direct conversation.

What is Project Synapse?

Project Synapse is my multi-phase sovereign security engineering program — an evolving architecture that grew from a basic detection prototype into a national-scale platform with six specialized domains (Vector, Umbra, Spectra, Holmes, Axiom, Aegis). See the full breakdown here.
