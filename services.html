Services

Focused engagements for teams that need rigorous, practical security work.

What I Offer

Service areas

⚙

Security Automation

Designing and building automated pipelines for detection, triage, enrichment, and response. This includes SIEM/SOAR integration, custom scripting, and workflow engineering — built to reduce manual workload without introducing brittle dependencies.

▸

Pipeline & Workflow Design

Architecting structured data and operational workflows for security teams: from log ingestion pipelines to multi-stage alert handling and case management processes. Emphasis on maintainability and clear ownership.

◈

Security Architecture Review

Reviewing system and application designs for security gaps — covering network segmentation, access control models, secrets management, and third-party integrations. Delivered as structured findings with actionable recommendations.

⬡

Detection Engineering

Building and tuning detection rules, alert logic, and coverage maps for specific threat models. Includes rule lifecycle management: versioning, testing, staging, and deployment processes that keep detections reliable over time.

⌥

Tooling Development

Writing practical internal tools: integrations, automation scripts, parsers, enrichment modules, and security-aware CI/CD components. Code is documented, testable, and designed to be maintained by whoever comes next.

◻

Process & Runbook Design

Documenting and structuring security operations processes — incident response playbooks, escalation paths, and on-call runbooks — so that institutional knowledge is captured and teams can operate under pressure without ambiguity.

Engagement

How engagements work

Work is structured as scoped projects or retainers, depending on scope and duration. Engagement volume stays limited to preserve technical quality and direct execution.

Discovery

Scoped Proposal

A clear written scope of work with deliverables, timeline, and cost. No ambiguity, no hidden scope.

Delivery

Work delivered iteratively with regular check-ins. Handoff includes documentation and transition support.
